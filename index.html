Welcome to LegacyXperia Project homepage.

LegacyXperia Project is offering unofficial builds of CyanogenMod for legacy Sony Ericsson msm7x30 devices. It is an effort to join all developer forces to bring a stable rom on these devices which are dropped from official CM after cm-10.

Supported devices:

Currently only mogami devices are supported. zeus devices don't have a 3.4 kernel port yet so it's not possible to support them, no active developer has this device.

List of supported devices & links to xda support & development threads:

  • Xperia Arc/Arc S (anzu) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Live with Walkman (coconut) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Neo V (haida) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Neo (hallon) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Pro (iyokan) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Mini Pro (mango) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Active (satsuma) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Mini (smultron) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
  • Xperia Ray (urushi) cm-10.1 , cm-10.2
