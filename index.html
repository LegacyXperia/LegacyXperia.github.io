Welcome to LegacyXperia Project homepage.

LegacyXperia Project is offering unofficial builds of CyanogenMod for legacy Sony Ericsson msm7x30 devices. It is an effort to join all developer forces to bring a stable rom on these devices which are dropped from official CM after cm-10.

Supported devices:

Currently only mogami devices are supported. Support for zeus devices is in progress.

List of supported devices:

  • Xperia Arc/Arc S (anzu)
  • Live with Walkman (coconut)
  • Xperia Neo V (haida)
  • Xperia Neo (hallon)
  • Xperia Pro (iyokan)
  • Xperia Mini Pro (mango)
  • Xperia Active (satsuma)
  • Xperia Mini (smultron)
  • Xperia Ray (urushi)
